About Me

I'm an aspiring cybersecurity professional passionate about ethical hacking and penetration testing. As I embark on this journey, I invite you to join me in sharing my learning experiences and insights.

I recently completed the Google Cybersecurity Course, earning a Professional Certificate that equipped me with foundational knowledge and practical skills. This experience has fueled my determination to prepare for the CompTIA Security+ and Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) certification.

My next goal is to achieve the Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) certification, which will deepen my understanding of ethical hacking methodologies and improve my ability to defend against evolving cyber threats.

Operating Systems

Proficient in Windows, Linux, MacOS, ChromeOS, and Unix, with hands-on experience in distributions like Kali Linux, Red Hat, and Debian.

Networking

Strong grasp of network concepts and protocols (TCP/IP, HTTP, DNS), with experience in configuring firewalls, understanding VPNs, and knowledge of cloud networking.

Programming Languages

Foundational understanding in Python, HTML, SQL, and Bash/Shell scripting, with plans to expand to Ruby, Rust, Go, PHP, and JavaScript.

Intrusion Detection

Familiar with various IDS solutions like Snort, Suricata, Zeek and OSSEC, and skilled in both signature-based and anomaly detection techniques.
